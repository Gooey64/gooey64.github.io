Why rent a platypus?

Renting a platypus offers a one-of-a-kind experience that’s sure to delight and surprise. Whether for a special event, an educational program, or simply to bring a unique touch to your day, a platypus provides an unforgettable encounter with one of nature’s most extraordinary creatures. Its playful behavior and distinct appearance captivate people of all ages, making it a perfect choice for anyone seeking a fun, engaging, and memorable experience. From sparking curiosity to promoting joy, renting a platypus brings a rare opportunity to connect with wildlife in an exciting and meaningful way.


How do I handle a platypus?

Handling instructions will be provided, and you will receive safety instructions from the platypus’ supervisor when renting, but here's a quick intro:

  • Always approach a platypus slowly and calmly, giving them space to get comfortable.
  • When lifting, cradle their body gently, supporting their webbed feet and tail.
  • Remember, the males have a venomous spur on their hind legs, so it's best to let experts handle them directly.
  • Platypuses love to swim, so make sure they have access to water for a dip.
  • Respect their boundaries, and you'll enjoy a safe and memorable experience with these fascinating animals!
